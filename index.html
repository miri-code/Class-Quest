About Us

Class Quest was created with love by a fellow student, Miriam Fahim, an undergrad at Georgia Tech.
After her first day as a transfer student—sweaty, confused, and hopelessly lost trying to find both her classrooms and her way through the labyrinth inside the buildings—Miriam had enough.
By the end of the week, after so many "I wish I knew that" moments, she decided to fix the problem herself.
We hope that you're dry and informed on all your first days!
