Welcome to Class Quest! Here’s a guide on how to navigate and use our app effectively.

Getting Started

Using Class Quest is simple! Just enter your class number and building name, and the app will provide the level, building, and exact area of the room on campus. It also highlights landmarks near the room that stand out, and gives details on what's nearby, like parking decks, restaurants, and offices. You’ll even see the hours of operation for the building and student ratings that tell you if the room is easy to find..

Features

Explore the different features of the app and how they can benefit you.

Planning your schedule? Queue your classes with the professor’s name, course number, and time of day. Plus, our accessibility section shows if the room is close to an elevator or has a wheelchair-friendly entrance, making navigation even easier!
